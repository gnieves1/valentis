About Us

Our food is prepared fresh with quality ingredients imported from Italy and New York. Our concept has always been to provide excellent food service in a clean and friendly environment. Our biggest assets are our employees’ dedication and passion for food. We hope you enjoy your dinning experience as much as we enjoy serving you.

History

Valenti’s Italian restaurant is a casual, full service restaurant, established in 1996. It is family owned and operated by a third generation Italian from a family that moved from Italy to Long Island New York. "Our passion for Italian food has enabled us to create some of the best pastas and pizzas. Since opening in 1996 we, have been committed to providing quality food inspired by Italian tradition." -Adam Valenti
